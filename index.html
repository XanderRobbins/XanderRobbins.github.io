About Me

University of Florida student majoring in Mathematics, Computer Science, and Economics, graduating 3 years early in 2027. Focuses: probability, statistics, finance, with a focus on applying rigorous mathematical models to real-world datasets. I've completed advanced coursework in operating systems, linear algebra, data structures, and probability, and I'm currently studying real analysis and abstract algebra.

Outside the classroom, I work with a student-run quantitative hedge fund, where I build and test models using Python and C++. I'm driven by deep problem-solving, independent research, and developing quantitative tools that bridge theory and practice.
